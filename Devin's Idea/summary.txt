BAR APP — QUICK SUMMARY

Pitch: BeReal for nightlife. Free for users. Bars pay for analytics.

How it works:
- BARS post the data (cover, music, deals, vibe) from their dashboard — takes 30 sec
- USERS just confirm/correct with one tap, or check in with one tap ("I'm here")
- Photos/details are optional bonus — never required
- Data wipes nightly at close (ephemeral, fresh every night)
- Map view shows which bars are hot tonight
- Bars subscribe ($49–$149/mo) to see analytics on their venue

Why users open the app (selfishly useful):
- Post to unlock the map (BeReal-style gate)
- "Your Night, Archived" — personal scrapbook of every night out
- Friends Out Tonight — see which friends are out and where (opt-in mutual)
- Streaks/badges (light gamification)

Notifications (the engine):
- 9pm Fri/Sat push: "What's your night looking like?" (BeReal-style ritual)
- "Sarah just checked in at The Tap House" (friends-driven FOMO)
- Smart bar pings: "$3 shots til 11pm, 0.4 mi away" (opt-in only)
- End-of-night recap: "Your night: 2 bars, photos saved to archive"

Money:
- Bar subscriptions (main revenue)
- Sponsored placements ($199+/mo for premium pin/feature)
- Rideshare partnership cut (passive, add later)
- NO ads, NO user subscription — users 100% free

Safety (critical):
- NO user selfies in public feed (back camera only, venue/crowd photos)
- Friends-only for any identifying content
- Public data is aggregate ("Bar X is packed") not individual
- 18+ verification, on-device face blur, fast block/report
- Ephemeral posts double as a safety feature

Tech stack:
- React Native + Expo (mobile, iOS + Android)
- Supabase (backend, real-time, auth, storage)
- Mapbox (maps)
- Next.js (bar dashboard web app)
- Stripe (bar subscriptions)

Name shortlist:
LastCall, OnTap, Tapped, BarPulse, HopIn

Legal (no patent, but...):
- Can't patent the idea (software business concepts not patentable post-Alice 2014)
- DO trademark the name (~$250) once picked
- Form an LLC ($50–500)
- Privacy Policy + ToS day one (Termly/Iubenda ~$10/mo)

Data storage:
- Supabase (Postgres + Auth + Storage + Realtime, all-in-one)
- $0 free tier at launch, $25/mo at 10K users, ~$200–500/mo at 100K
- Cloudflare R2 for photos at scale (cheaper than S3)
- US-East region, GDPR/CCPA compliant from day one

App Store launch:
- Apple Developer: $99/year, 24–48hr review
- Google Play: $25 one-time, few hours review
- 17+ rating required (alcohol/nightlife)
- MUST have: report system, block system, moderation, ToS, clear location-use copy
- Build with Expo EAS, ship to TestFlight first
- Total launch cost: ~$150
- Timeline code-done → live: ~1–2 weeks iOS, ~3–5 days Android
